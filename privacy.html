How this site works

SkyDeals provides information and guidance about finding cheap flights and deals. We do not sell tickets directly; we link to third‑party services that handle booking and payment.

Affiliate links

Some links on this site are affiliate links. When you click these links and book a flight or travel product, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

The destination and pricing are controlled by the external booking partner, not by SkyDeals. We have no control over how that partner handles your data.

Data we collect

At this time, SkyDeals:

  • Does not collect or store personal information beyond what your browser sends in standard web traffic (e.g., IP address, pages visited).

Your choices

If you prefer not to support this site via affiliate links, you can:
