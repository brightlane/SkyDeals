How far in advance should I book a flight?

For most domestic flights, booking 1–3 months in advance is ideal. For international routes, consider 2–6 months to catch the best prices.

What are the cheapest days to fly?

Tuesdays and Wednesdays often show the lowest fares. Weekends can be more expensive, especially on popular routes.

How can I get cheaper international flights?

Try:

  • Booking in shoulder or off‑peak seasons.
  • Using alternative airports near your city or destination.
  • Checking a variety of dates and airlines.
