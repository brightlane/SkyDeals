Which regions are the cheapest from the US?

Some of the most affordable international routes from the United States often go to:

  • Mexico and the Caribbean – short flights and competitive prices.
  • Central America – budget‑friendly destinations like Costa Rica and Panama.
  • Europe on low‑season weekends – consider flying in shoulder months (e.g., September, October, April, May).

How far in advance should you book?

For most international trips:

  • Book 2–6 months in advance.
  • Avoid booking too close to major holidays or big events in your destination.
