How far in advance should you book?

For most domestic routes, the sweet spot is 1–3 months in advance. For international flights, consider booking 2–6 months ahead to catch early‑bird discounts.

Best days of the week to book

Studies and fare trackers show that Tuesdays and Wednesdays often have the lowest fares. Weekends usually come with a small price bump, especially for popular routes.

Peak vs off‑peak travel

Flying during school holidays, major events, and long weekends often means higher prices. If you can shift your trip by a few days, you may save significantly.

How to use your route‑specific examples

The same timing rules apply to:

  • New York → Miami – try to book 1–2 months in advance.
  • Philadelphia → Orlando – avoid peak holiday weeks for the best rates.
